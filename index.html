Piece Abilities

Promotion: Reach the opposite end of the board to promote pieces and gain special abilities! Normal pawns promote to non-evolvable pieces. The options are queen, rook, bischop and knight.

Commander: Starts on a random back-rank file. Moves like a King + Pawn. Promotes to an Assassin or any other evolved piece.

Assassin (Evolved Commander): Can teleport onto any enemy pawn and capture it.

Tower (Evolved Rook): Can use its turn to toggle an invulnerability shield on/off. When shielded, it cannot move or be captured, and ally pieces can move and attack through it!

Ninja (Evolved Bishop): If your original Queen/Monarch is captured, the Ninja can sacrifice itself to instantly revive the Queen/Monarch.

Dragon (Evolved Knight): Can jump/teleport to any empty square on the opponent's half of the board.

Monarch (Evolved Original Queen): Can teleport to any empty square directly adjacent to your King.

Tyran (Evolved King): Can use its turn to instantly spawn a new Pawn on any empty square of its starting rank.

Point System

The score displayed during the game is purely to help you gauge the overall strength of each player's board. Pieces are valued as follows:

  • Pawn: 1 point
  • Knight & Bishop: 3 points
  • Rook: 5 points
  • Commander: 7 points
  • Queen: 9 points
  • Any Evolved Piece: 12 points
